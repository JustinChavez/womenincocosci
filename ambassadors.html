Ambassadors

I'm hoping this page will serve as both an assemblage of young people starting out in the field, and also a resource list of people who would be happy to talk about their experiences. I'm currently in the process of asking friends if they would be willing to be included on this page; if you'd be kind enough to be included, please shoot me an email (mgates[at]berkeley[dot]edu)!
